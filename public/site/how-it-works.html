HOW IT WORKS

Five steps, then it just runs.

You never talk to twelve vendors again. Your team requests items in the portal; we order, consolidate at our San Diego facility, and deliver to your bench — then bill once a month.

THE PROCESS ENROLL → BILL
01

Enroll

START HERE

Send us a real order from your lab — the messier the better. We’ll load it into the portal and show you exactly how we’d run it across 200+ vendors, so you can see the workflow before you sign anything.

02

Onboarding

ABOUT ONE WEEK

Sign the agreement and establish a line of credit through a security deposit. We set your team up in the portal, map your usual items to their best sources, and agree on a delivery rhythm that fits your lab.

03

Request

IN THE PORTAL

Anyone on your team requests items in the portal. You see availability and lead time on every line, set what matters most on each order — speed or a specific lot — and approve. Nothing ships without your sign-off.

04

We order & consolidate

AT OUR FACILITY

We place every approved order under our vendor accounts. Everything ships to our San Diego facility — not your loading dock — where we receive it, check it against packing slips, and combine it into one delivery.

05

We deliver, we bill once

TO YOUR BENCH

Your consolidated order arrives at your bench on your schedule, cold chain respected. At the end of the month you get one itemized invoice — every line at vendor price, our fee its own line, receipts pulled on your behalf.

WHY CONSOLIDATION

Your freezer isn’t a receiving department.

Small labs lose whole afternoons to receiving: signing for boxes one at a time, chasing a missing tube on a six-line packing slip, discovering the dry ice ran out on Friday’s delivery that arrived Monday.

We take that on. Every vendor ships to us; we verify contents against your order, flag shorts and backorders in the portal, and bring everything to your lab in one scheduled drop — cold items last off the truck.

GOT QUESTIONS?

The short answers.

How often do you deliver?

We run consolidated routes across San Diego County on Tuesdays and Thursdays, and urgent items go out same-day or next-day when the science can’t wait. You set the rhythm during onboarding.

Is there an order minimum?

No. A single box of tips rides the same route as a freezer restock. Consolidation is the point — small orders are exactly what we absorb.

What does it cost?

You pay what the vendor charges — we never mark up items. Our service fee is flat, quoted up front, and appears as its own line on your monthly invoice.

Whose vendor accounts are used?

Ours. You get the pricing leverage of 200+ vendor relationships without opening a single account — and if you leave, your purchase history exports with you.

Can you handle regulated materials?

Yes — controlled-material paperwork, chain of custody for toxicology work, and USP-grade documentation with lot traceability for 503B compounding pharmacies.
