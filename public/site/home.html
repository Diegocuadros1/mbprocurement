02 — THE OPERATION

Built around the county’s lab clusters.

From Torrey Pines to Carlsbad, our routes run where San Diego does its science. Orders from a dozen vendors arrive at our facility, get checked and combined, and reach your bench with a driver you know — not twelve tracking numbers.

The software holds it together: requests, side-by-side vendor pricing, approvals, documents, and one itemized monthly bill, in one portal your whole team shares.
